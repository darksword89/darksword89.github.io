How to Play

  • Move: Arrow keys (desktop) or the on-screen D-Pad (mobile).
  • Collect: Pots to gain score.
  • Avoid: Monsters (they cost lives).
  • Power-up: At 100 score, Thor’s Hammer activates.
  • Boss: At 300 score, the boss appears and chases you.
  • Difficulty: Every 30 points, monster speed increases by 3%.

Tip: On phones, tap Start to enable audio.
